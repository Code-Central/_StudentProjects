Lunar Souls

The current game I am working on is one called Lunar Souls. You wake up to the God of Memories sitting in your dining room. Everything fades to black as you wake up and start your adventure. You must go through different dimensions and assisting multiple gods and goddess to take down their brother. You have to collect the gems of the Goddess Light and God Night in order to combine them and take back the multiverse once and for all.

Culinary Containment

Culinary Containment is a cute fantasy game about finding and nurturing a variety of mutated animals. These once cute little animals were combined with a variety of foods turning them into monsters! While your character is out exploring you can tame, fight, or chat with a variety of these foodimals! Collect them all and help bring your world back to peace.
